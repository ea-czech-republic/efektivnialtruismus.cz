Example topic areas

Biosecurity
With a steep progress in our knowledge of synthetic biology and decreasing prices of technologies used to create pathogens, the risk and severity of pandemics from both natural and engineered pathogens will likely increase and cause millions or even billions of deaths.

Global Priorities Research

What are the most important problems we should focus on to improve the world the most? How should we allocate our resources to do the most good? More insight into these questions could largely influence what we will do and how successful we will be in improving the world.

AI governance

Developments in artificial intelligence algorithms will likely have a large influence on many domains of our societies and lives. Research into how we should govern the creation and deployment of such developments will help us capture the benefits and mitigate the risks stemming from abrupt changes.
